WHO'S THERE? THE BOYNEXTDOOR!

BOYNEXTDOOR (보이넥스트도어) (can be shorten as BND) is an South Korean boy group under KOZ Entertainment who debuted on May 30, 2023 with the single album “WHO!“. The members are JAEHYUN, SUNGHO, RIWOO, TAESAN, LEEHAN, and WOONHAK. ‘BOYNEXTDOOR’ Meaning: They’re the boys who live next door. They will be singing songs that a lot of people can hopefully relate to in their daily lives, as friendly as their name.

WHO! is the debut single album by boy group BOYNEXTDOOR. It was released on May 30, 2023 with “But I Like You”, “One and Only” and “Serenade” as the album's title tracks. The physical album comes in three versions: WHO ver., Crunch ver. and Weverse Albums ver.
